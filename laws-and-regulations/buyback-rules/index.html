Redemption (Buyback) Operations Regulations

Treasury Buyback Regulations (31 CFR Part 375)

Treasury’s securities redemption (buyback) operations regulations set out the terms and conditions by which we may redeem outstanding, unmatured marketable Treasury securities.

Amendments to Treasury’s buyback regulations

Buyback rule update (March 2026)

On March 30, 2026, we amended the regulations to reflect expanded direct offer submission eligibility, update the certification statements to participate in buyback operations, enhance clarity, and make conforming changes to several sections of the buyback regulations to reflect Treasury’s current practices. View comment letter received in response to the proposed rule.

Historical information

On January 19, 2000, we issued the final rules that set out the terms and conditions by which we may redeem (buy back) outstanding, unmatured marketable Treasury securities.
