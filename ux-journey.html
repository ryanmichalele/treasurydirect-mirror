Intro & Eligibility

Screen
★ Secure • Expected time: ~10 minutes

Before starting, make sure you have the following ready:

  • ✓ Taxpayer Identification Number / Social Security Number
  • ✓ U.S. address of record
  • ✓ Checking or savings account (your own)
  • ✓ Bank routing number and account number
  • ✓ Valid email address
  • ✓ Browser with 128-bit encryption
  • ✓ You must be 18 or older for a primary account
ⓘ
Why this matters:
TreasuryDirect is a United States government financial system. All information is verified against government records. Your SSN/TIN, address, and banking details must match what’s on file with the IRS and your financial institution.

Confirmation & Next Steps

Screen
✔️

Application Submitted Successfully

Your TreasuryDirect Individual account application has been received.

📅
What happens next: TreasuryDirect verifies your personal information against government records. Most verifications complete within minutes. You will receive an email with your account number and next steps.

✓ If verification succeeds

Your account opens immediately. You will receive your account number and can log in to buy bonds, manage your portfolio, and set up recurring purchases.

⚠ If verification fails

You may need to complete a printable authentication form (PDF) and mail it with copies of identifying documents to the TreasuryDirect processing centre. Processing takes 2–4 weeks.
