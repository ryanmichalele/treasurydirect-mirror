The Fiscal Service Announces Activity for Securities in the STRIPS Program for May 2026

FOR RELEASE AT 3:00 PM

June 4, 2026

The Bureau of the Fiscal Service announced activity during the month of May 2026 for securities within the Separate Trading of Registered Interest and Principal of Securities program (STRIPS).

Dollar Amounts In Thousands
Principal Outstanding (Eligible Securities)
$23,312,484,477
Held in Unstripped Form
$22,695,004,850
Held in Stripped Form
$617,479,626
Reconstituted in May
$24,297,807

The accompanying table gives a breakdown of STRIPS activity by individual loan description. The balances in this table are subject to audit and subsequent revision. These monthly figures are included in Table V of the Monthly Statement of The Public Debt, entitled "Holdings of Treasury Securities in Stripped Form."

The STRIPS Table along with the new Monthly Statement of The Public Debt is available on Fiscal Service's Internet site at https://fiscaldata.treasury.gov/. A wide range of information about the public debt and Treasury securities is available at https://www.treasurydirect.gov/.
